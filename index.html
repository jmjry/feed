What is Feed?

Feed is a brand new restaurant opening in the heart of Oxford January 2022. Our kitchen team is headed up by award-winning chef Lewis Ryan, well-known within the restaurant scene. In addition to our incredible team, we're partnering with local farms, fishmongers, butchers and other businesses to ensure we are as sustainable, supportive and succesful as possible. We are proud to cater for all dietry requirements and encourage anyone with them to speak with our team to find the perfect meal for you. Bookings are now open for January 2022 and our first menu is available for viewing. Thank you for your support, we look forward to seeing you in January.
